How to use:

	left click:		toggle mouse look
	W/A/S/D/T/G:		move
	arrows:			turn
	shift W/A/S/D/T/G:	move fast
	T/G:			move up/down
	wheel/+/-:		change fov
	ctrl wheel/+/-:		change speed

	F1:	rectilinear projection
	F2:	cylinder panorama
	F3:	vertical cylinder panorama
	F4:	spherical panrama
	F5:	fuzzy projection
	tab:	change renderer (e.g. toggle between software and OpenGL)

	0:	random textured or solid polygon
	1:	flatten polygons
	8:	20 random textured or solid polygons
	9:	200 flattened polygons
